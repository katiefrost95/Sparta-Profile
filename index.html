About Me



I am 22 years old and have lived in Croydon, London my whole life. In July 2017 I graduated from the University of Birmingham with a 2:1 in American literature and History. My course was 4 years long including a year studying abroad at the University of Maine in America. This was a great cultural experience and I used this as an opportunity to travel the states.


I have since chosen to to pursue a career in the tech industry. I first got a taste of coding through Code First:Girls. For this we met once a week for a period of 8 weeks learning the basics of html and CSS. The couse culminated in a competition where we created our own website in groups. I am currently a trainee IT Consultant at Sparta Global and will finish my training on January 24th 2018. In the training period at Sparta Global we are taught to code in html, css, javascript, and ruby. Once I have finished my training I will the begin my career as a software developer.
